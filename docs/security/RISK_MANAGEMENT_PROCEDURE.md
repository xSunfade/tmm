# Risk Management Procedure (TMM)

Version: 1.0  
Owner: Security Lead  
Review cadence: Monthly

## 1. Risk lifecycle

1. Identify risk (architecture changes, incidents, audits, vendor changes).
2. Assess likelihood and impact.
3. Assign owner and mitigation action.
4. Track due date and status.
5. Reassess residual risk after mitigation.

## 2. Scoring model

- Likelihood: 1 (Rare) to 5 (Almost certain)
- Impact: 1 (Low) to 5 (Critical)
- Risk score = Likelihood x Impact

Severity bands:

- 1-5: Low
- 6-10: Medium
- 11-15: High
- 16-25: Critical

## 3. Response expectations

- Critical: mitigation plan within 24h, executive visibility.
- High: mitigation plan within 3 business days.
- Medium: mitigation plan within 10 business days.
- Low: backlog with periodic review.

## 4. Cadence

- Monthly risk review meeting.
- Quarterly risk trend and control effectiveness review.
- Ad hoc review after major incidents.

## 5. Evidence

- Risk register updates and meeting notes.
- Issue links for mitigation tasks.
- Closure evidence for resolved risks.
